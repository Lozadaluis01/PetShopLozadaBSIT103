Day 1: Setting Up the Shop

January 1, 2024

Today was a big day! I officially started setting up LozadaLuis Animal Shop. It's been a dream of mine to provide a trusted store for pets, pet supplies, and expert care tips. I spent the day organizing the initial inventory of high-quality pet food and supplies. I also set up the website to start sharing helpful pet care advice.

Day 2: First Customers!

January 2, 2024

I had my first customers today! It was so exciting to help pet owners find the perfect products for their furry friends. One customer was looking for the best dog breed for their family, and it was rewarding to share some tips. Another was concerned about their cat's nutrition, so I recommended some of our top-selling nutritious foods. It's great to see my passion for animals translating into helping others.

Day 7: Expanding Our Offerings

January 7, 2024

I'm working on expanding the shop's offerings. I want to make sure we have everything pet owners need. Today, I focused on researching and sourcing new products, including items for small animals and aquarium setups. I also added a new section to the website with essential cat care tips and bird-keeping basics. There's a lot to do, but I'm energized by the progress we're making.
